The Listening Post

A shortwave receiver. Everything on it is transmitting right now — nothing is recorded.

  • Tune — drag the glass or the dial. Fling it and the dial coasts. The wheel is fine tuning; arrow keys work too.
  • The band is crowded — most lines are strangers passing through. The steady, named ones reward holding: lock one and it's pencilled into the log (L).
  • Zoom the window with Z — narrow the span and the keying resolves into readable dots and dashes.
  • Three bands — 1 2 3. The strip at the top of the glass is the whole band; tap it to jump.
  • Some of the band only wakes after dark. Your dark — and some nights, something crosses that isn't there the next.
